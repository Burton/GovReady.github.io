--- layout: control permalink: /CCE-27090-0/ name: CCE-27090-0 title: Mount Remote Filesystems with nodev ident: CCE-27090-0 severity: medium ---

Legitimate device files should only exist in the /dev directory. NFS mounts should not present device files to users.

Failing to mount remote filesystems with nodev is of medium severity.

View all 2 Mount Remote Filesystems with Restrictive Options rules.

Technical Description

Add the nodev option to the fourth column of /etc/fstab for the line which controls mounting of any NFS mounts.
