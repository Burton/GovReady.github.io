--- layout: control permalink: /CCE-26720-3/ name: CCE-26720-3 title: Add noexec Option to /tmp ident: CCE-26720-3 severity: low ---

Allowing users to execute binaries from world-writable directories such as /tmp should never be necessary in normal operation and can expose the system to potential compromise.

Failing to add noexec option to /tmp is of low severity.

View all 11 Restrict Partition Mount Options rules.

Technical Description

The noexec mount option can be used to prevent binaries from being executed out of /tmp. Add the noexec option to the fourth column of /etc/fstab for the line which controls mounting of /tmp.
