--- layout: control permalink: /CCE-26972-0/ name: CCE-26972-0 title: Mount Remote Filesystems with nosuid ident: CCE-26972-0 severity: medium ---

NFS mounts should not present suid binaries to users. Only vendor-supplied suid executables should be installed to their default location on the local filesystem.

Failing to mount remote filesystems with nosuid is of medium severity.

View all 2 Mount Remote Filesystems with Restrictive Options rules.

Technical Description

Add the nosuid option to the fourth column of /etc/fstab for the line which controls mounting of any NFS mounts.
