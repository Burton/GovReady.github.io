Failing to disable bluetooth service is of medium severity.

Rationale

Disabling the bluetooth service prevents the system from attempting connections to Bluetooth devices, which entails some security risk. Nevertheless, variation in this risk decision may be expected due to the utility of Bluetooth connectivity and its limited range.

via FISMA SSG. View all 4 Disable Wireless Through Software Configuration rules.

Technical Description

The bluetooth service can be disabled with the following command: # chkconfig bluetooth off 

# service bluetooth stop
