Set Account Expiration Parameters Rules

Accounts can be configured to be automatically disabled after a certain time period, meaning that they will require administrator interaction to become usable again. Expiration of accounts after inactivity can be set for all accounts by default and also on a per-account basis, such as for accounts that are known to be temporary.
