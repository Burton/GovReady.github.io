Disable Automatic Configuration Rules

Disable the system's acceptance of router advertisements and redirects by adding or correcting the following line in (note that this does not disable sending router solicitations):

1 CCE-27164-3 sysctl_net_ipv6_conf_default_accept_ra low disabling_ipv6_autoconfig (2)
2 CCE-27166-8 sysctl_ipv6_default_accept_redirects medium disabling_ipv6_autoconfig (2)
