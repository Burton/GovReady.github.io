--- layout: control permalink: /CCE-26691-6/ name: CCE-26691-6 title: Record Attempts to Alter Logon and Logout Events ident: CCE-26691-6 severity: low ---

Manual editing of these files may indicate nefarious activity, such as an attacker attempting to remove evidence of an intrusion.

Failing to record attempts to alter logon and logout events is of low severity.

View all 14 Configure auditd Rules for Comprehensive Auditing rules.

Technical Description

The audit system already collects login info for all users and root. To watch for attempted manual edits of files involved in storing logon events, add the following to /etc/audit/audit.rules: 

-w /var/log/faillog -p wa -k logins 
-w /var/log/lastlog -p wa -k logins
