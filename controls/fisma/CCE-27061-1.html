--- layout: control permalink: /CCE-27061-1/ name: CCE-27061-1 title: Disable Advanced Configuration and Power Interface (acpid) ident: CCE-27061-1 severity: low ---

ACPI support is highly desirable for systems in some network roles, such as laptops or desktops. For other systems, such as servers, it may permit accidental or trivially achievable denial of service situations and disabling it is appropriate.

Failing to disable advanced configuration and power interface (acpid) is of low severity.

View all 24 Base Services rules.

Technical Description

The Advanced Configuration and Power Interface Daemon (acpid) dispatches ACPI events (such as power/reset button depressed) to userspace programs. The acpid service can be disabled with the following command: # chkconfig acpid off
