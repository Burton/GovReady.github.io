--- layout: control permalink: /CCE-27267-4/ name: CCE-27267-4 title: Disable Certmonger Service (certmonger) ident: CCE-27267-4 severity: low ---

The services provided by certmonger may be essential for systems fulfilling some roles a PKI infrastructure, but its functionality is not necessary for many other use cases.

Failing to disable certmonger service (certmonger) is of low severity.

View all 24 Base Services rules.

Technical Description

Certmonger is a D-Bus based service that attempts to simplify interaction with certifying authorities on networks which use public-key infrastructure. It is often combined with Red Hat's IPA (Identity Policy Audit) security information management solution to aid in the management of certificates. The certmonger service can be disabled with the following command: # chkconfig certmonger off
