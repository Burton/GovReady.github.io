About

FISMA is the mandated risk management framework to protect information on Federal Government non-Defense IT systems.

FISMA recommended controls for IT systems is described in NIST Special Publication 800-53. Within government, people refer to these controls as "800-53's". Mapping the FISMA controls to a piece of software is called Common Configuration Enumeration and produces a list of uniquely-identified "CCE's".

This page lists the 800-53 {{ page.severity | capitalize }} CCE's for RHEL6/CentOS6/Fedora Linux.
