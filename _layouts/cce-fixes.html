FISMA Controls CCE List and Fixes for RHEL6/CentOS6/Fedora Linux

About

FISMA is the mandated risk management framework to protect information on Federal Government IT systems.

FISMA recommended controls for non-DoD IT systems is described in NIST Special Publication 800-53. Within government, people refer to these controls as "the 800-53's". Mapping the FISMA controls to a piece of software is called Common Configuration Enumeration and produces a list of uniquely-identified "CCE's".

This page lists 800-53 CCE's for RHEL6/CentOS6/Fedora Linux with cross-references to existing community-maintained fixes.
