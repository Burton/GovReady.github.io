About

390 FISMA-related configuration rules to improve security of RHEL6/CentOS/Fedora Linux systems described by NIST Special Publication 800-53 controls with cross-references to know remediation scripts. CCE stands for Common Configuration Enumaration.

FISMA is the mandated risk management framework for protecting information on Federal Government IT systems.

An official list of FISMA recommended controls for non-DOD IT systems is described in large part by NIST Special Publication 800-53. Within government, people informally refer to the controls as "the 800-53's".

Platform specific implementations of the 800-53's are formally known as Common Configuration Enumeration and informally refered to as "CCE's".
